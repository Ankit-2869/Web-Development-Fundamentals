Planets of the Milky Way Galaxy

  • Mercury

    The smallest and innermost planet in the Solar System.

    Type: Terrestrial

  • Venus

    The second planet from the Sun, known for its dense, toxic atmosphere.

    Type: Terrestrial

  • Earth

    The only astronomical object where life is known to exist.

    Type: Terrestrial

  • Mars

    The "Red Planet," famous for its reddish appearance and iron oxide dust.

    Type: Terrestrial

  • Jupiter

    The largest planet in the Solar System, a massive gas giant.

    Type: Gas Giant

  • Saturn

    Recognizable by its extensive and beautiful system of rings.

    Type: Gas Giant

  • Uranus

    An ice giant that rotates on its side, unique among the planets.

    Type: Ice Giant

  • Neptune

    The farthest known planet from the Sun and an ice giant.

    Type: Ice Giant
